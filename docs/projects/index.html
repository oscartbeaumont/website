Another School Social

A Friend and I ran sound and lighting for another social at school. It was held in an auditorium that already had lighting equipment so we ran two lighting “loops” both with different controllers. The existing lighting in the room was run off a Smartfade ML and my friends custom built lighting bar and lights were powered by QLC Plus. The Sound was run using a Si Expression and 4 EV SX300’s.
